The Ubud Rafting

Adventure awaits

Grab your oars and get ready to rage with this exhilarating rafting adventure on the spectacular Ayung River on the west side of Ubud.

Our trained and experienced guides will pilot your safety-equipped rafts as you navigate through the class II & III rapids set to a backdrop of wild unspoiled rainforest, towering gorges and magnificent rice paddy terraces.

Whether you are 6 or 65, a natural or a novice, our white water rafting is safe, fun and a breathtaking experience to be enjoyed by the whole family and friends.

Our facility

White Water Rafting tours in Bali are not all the same. The Ubud Rafting uses equipment that is approved to international standards and operated by professionally trained guides.

Our tour includes:

  • The longest rafting distance - 10km
  • Air-conditioned return transfers (optional)
  • Changing room & lockers
  • Hot showers & towels
  • Buffet lunch with Balinese menu
  • Emergency accident insurance
